Tailored Delivery Frameworks

To match the unique scale, pace, and goals of your projects, we offer three proven agile team structures. Each is designed to foster collaboration, accelerate delivery, and provide clear ownership.

Squad Model

Autonomous, multi-disciplinary teams dedicated to a long-term mission. Squads align within Tribes for coordination and use Chapters for skill growth and consistency.

Agile POD Model

A small, self-sufficient team that operates like a mini-startup, with all roles needed for end-to-end ownership. Promotes focus, autonomy, and faster execution.

Scrum Teams

A structured, sprint-based framework for delivering incremental value. Comprises a Scrum Master, Product Owner, and developers, emphasizing feedback, adaptability, and continuous improvement.
